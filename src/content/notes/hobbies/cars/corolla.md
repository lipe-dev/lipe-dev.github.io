# Toyota Corolla Hybrid

The family car. A modern, efficient hybrid that handles daily transportation duties while sipping fuel.

## About the Car

Toyota's hybrid technology is proven and reliable. The Corolla Hybrid combines practicality with excellent fuel economy, making it perfect for family duties and commuting.

Key features:
- **Engine**: 1.8L Hybrid
- **Fuel Economy**: Excellent city MPG
- **Technology**: Toyota Safety Sense suite
- **Comfort**: Practical interior with good space

## Why a Hybrid

After years of working on older cars and dealing with maintenance, there's value in having a reliable, modern vehicle that just works. The Corolla Hybrid fills that role perfectly - it's the sensible choice that lets me focus my project energy on the [[toyota-bandeirante]] and [[chevette]].
